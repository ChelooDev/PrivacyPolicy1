ChacoViajes - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Quick Summary: ChacoViajes is a carpooling app for the Chaco region. We only collect information necessary to connect drivers and passengers. We use your camera only for profile photos, and we never share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Profile photo (optional), travel preferences
  • Travel Information: Trip details, pickup/dropoff locations, travel times

1.2 Camera Usage

We request camera permission for the following purposes:

  • Profile Photos: To take or select a profile picture for your account
  • Photo Selection: To choose existing photos from your device's gallery

Important: We do NOT access your camera without your explicit permission. You can use the app without providing a profile photo.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To connect you with other users for carpooling
  • To send you notifications about your trips and messages
  • To improve our app and services
  • To ensure safety and security of our platform

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information:

  • With other users when you create a trip or request to join a trip
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (like Firebase for data storage)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use secure authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Camera Permission Details

Why we need camera access:

  • To allow you to take a profile photo directly in the app
  • To select existing photos from your device's gallery

What we DON'T do:

  • We don't access your camera without your permission
  • We don't record or store camera data without your action
  • We don't use your camera for any purpose other than profile photos

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@chacoviajes.com
  • App: Use the contact form in the app settings

This privacy policy is effective as of December 2024 and applies to all users of the ChacoViajes app.
